Saama

2020-2022 I worked here. Here's what I did so far.

● Worked with the R&D team and built softwares, models and wrote research papers for accelerating clinical trials

● Built pipelines and wrote research papers(currently under review) for Question Summarisation. Question Summarisation is a dataset of clinical FAQs with summarised versions as targets. Usually the FAQs tend to be too long with unnecessary information in the Question which makes it harder to read quickly. The goal of the model is to predict the most accurate summary of the question.

● Built pipelines and wrote research papers in Natural Language processing for Bio-SimCSE. Currently, Sentence Transformers does not have a bio-medical version. Our research explored the baselines for creating such a model.

● Built and deployed to huggingface: Covid Question Answering

● Built an auto-mapping software for cross matching medical domain databases.

● Wrote software for a faster alternative to my own product TableQA.
